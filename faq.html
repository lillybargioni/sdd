FAQ

A look at the process:

All of our items are pre-washed so there should be little to no shrinkage. Everything is treated with color fixer and then dyed with high quality Procion MX fiber reactive dye to ensure vibrant colors that'll last for years to come. We offer clothing we enjoy wearing ourselves, only using the highest quality and most comfortable material.

How should I care for my garment?

We suggest washing separate for the first wash in case any dye hasn’t completely bonded to the fibers. Rayon items should be hand washed. Cotton items can be machine washed on cold. Hang dry for items with some spandex.

Shipping Info

We ship everything within the continental US via USPS. Most orders are shipped and delivered within 7 business days of purchase. International shipping can take up to one month and is a flat rate of $20.

Do you accept returns?

We do not accept returns HOWEVER please don’t hesitate to reach out if something doesn’t fit quite right. We want to ensure you absolutely love your piece and will work with you to find a solution if you’ve found it doesn’t quite work for you.
